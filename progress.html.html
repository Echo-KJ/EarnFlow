My Calisthenics Journey

From zero to gravity-defying — this is my story of strength, mindset, and motion.

2021 – Day 1

Started my journey. Could barely hold a plank. No equipment, no gym — just consistency and the will to change.

2022 – Breakthrough

Achieved my first 10 clean push-ups. Confidence soared. Learned that discipline beats motivation every time.

2023 – Athlete Mode

Hit a full handstand and began training others. Joined local calisthenics meets and found a community of warriors.

2025 – Legacy

Now building a movement — coaching, creating, and inspiring others to defy gravity with body and mind.
